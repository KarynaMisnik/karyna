Who I am

I started my work career as an English teacher. It was pleasure to teach children and see their success. Sometimes there were stressful and hard days but my energetic students always cheered me up.

I had to change my job because I moved to another city. I worked as a scientific consultant and had several duties. In general, I helped students and sometimes teachers to fill their knowledge gaps.

Eventually I moved to Finland and faced with the necessity to decide what to do next. I got an opportunity to study software development in Omnia.
I was always interested in video games and dreamed about being involved in the process of creating them. Programming to me was similar to magic.

I opened for myself the world of coding in Omnia. I started my path there from zero previous knowledge of IT field.

Now I feel I have achieved the level where I can show what I can create and I still want to learn a lot of new things.
